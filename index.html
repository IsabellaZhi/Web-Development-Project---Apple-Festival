Apple Festival

Time: Oct 01 - 03, 10:00 - 18:00

Location: Ithaca Commons

The 39th Annual Apple Harvest Festival is Back!

The best way to kick off New York Cider Week! Come and meet our fascinating apple growers, cider makers, and delectable cider donuts in Downtown Ithaca, New York! Apple Harvest Festival will invite a variety of farms that offer fresh apples and apple products, delectable baked goods, food trucks offering tasty bites, and a craft fair with artisans from around the region. There will also be live music and entertainment, plus a great blend of handicraft and book stores around Downtown Ithaca.
